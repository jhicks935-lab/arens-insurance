Core Offering

Supplemental Insurance

Supplemental insurance works alongside your existing health coverage to fill the financial gaps a health event creates. When illness or injury strikes, your health insurance covers medical costs — but what about your deductible, your lost wages, your mortgage, your childcare?

Supplemental insurance pays you directly, for whatever you need, whenever a covered event occurs. No receipts required. No restrictions on how you spend it.

Sean is a registered Aflac agent with over 12 years of experience in the Orange County market. Aflac is the #1 provider of supplemental insurance in the United States.

The core value: Supplemental insurance doesn't replace your health plan — it protects the financial side of a health event so you can focus entirely on recovery.

How It Works

1

A covered event occurs — accident, illness, hospital stay, diagnosis

2

You file a simple claim with supporting documentation

3

Aflac pays your cash benefit directly to you — fast

4

Use the money however you need — bills, mortgage, groceries, anything

Coverage for Every Risk

Accident

Accident Insurance

Pays benefits for covered injuries from accidents — ER visits, hospitalization, fractures, dislocations, and follow-up care. Because accidents don't give advance notice.

Cancer

Cancer Insurance

A lump-sum benefit paid upon first diagnosis of a covered cancer, plus benefits for ongoing treatment — surgery, chemo, radiation, and more. Covers the financial disruption, not just the medical bills.

Critical Illness

Critical Illness

A cash benefit paid upon diagnosis of covered critical illnesses including heart attack, stroke, and major organ failure. Use it however you need to.

Hospital

Hospital Indemnity

Daily cash benefit for each day you're hospitalized — regardless of what your health plan covers. Helps offset the costs that pile up when you're out of commission.

Disability

Short-Term Disability

Replaces a portion of your income if you're unable to work due to a covered illness or injury. Bills keep coming when your paycheck stops — this helps bridge the gap.

Life

Life Insurance

Term and whole life options to protect your family's financial future. We'll help you understand what amount of coverage makes sense for your situation.

Dental / Vision

Dental, Vision & Hearing

Affordable standalone dental, vision, and hearing coverage — especially valuable if your health plan doesn't include these or offers minimal benefits.

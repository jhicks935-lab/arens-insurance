About Sean & Lisa

Two careers. One mission. Protecting the families and businesses of Orange County with the depth of experience and personal attention they deserve.

Our Story

Where Expertise Meets Personal Service

Arens Insurance Services was founded on a simple observation: most people don't have an insurance advisor — they have a salesperson. There's a real difference. An advisor gets to know your situation, asks the questions that matter, and recommends what actually fits. A salesperson sells what's convenient.

Sean and Lisa Arens built this agency to be the former. After decades in the industry, Lisa brings the kind of experience that makes the difference when a claim needs to be filed or a confusing policy needs plain-English translation. Sean brings operational precision and sales leadership — the systems behind the service.

Together, they've created an agency that's small enough to know every client's name and experienced enough to handle any coverage situation. Based in Huntington Beach, serving Orange County and beyond.

Lisa Arens
Co-Founder & Insurance Advisor

Lisa Arens has spent more than 35 years in customer service — from her career as a travel agent to her work as an independent insurance advisor. She's seen nearly every situation a client can face, and she brings a relationship-first approach to every conversation.

When she transitioned from travel into insurance, she brought that same philosophy with her: understand what each person actually needs before recommending anything.

"Having a career that combines my professional knowledge with communicating socially with people is a huge plus to me," Lisa says. That's not marketing copy — it's how she's operated for three decades.

Sean Arens
Co-Founder & Agency Owner

Sean Arens has an unusual background for an insurance professional: before entering the industry, he spent years in aerospace manufacturing as a Production Manager at Zodiac Aerospace — a business where precision isn't optional and failure has real consequences.

He took that operational discipline into insurance, spending over a decade with Aflac (including as a District Sales Coordinator) and later as VP of Sales at Opportunity Insurance Agency before committing fully to Arens Insurance Services in 2025.

"This is how we provide for our family, while protecting yours." That's Sean's philosophy in one sentence — and it drives how he works with every client and employer partner.

Sean's Background

Aerospace Precision, Applied to Insurance

What makes Sean unusual in the insurance world isn't just his sales leadership experience — it's the 5 years he spent before insurance, at Zodiac Aerospace, turning around a production line that was failing by nearly every measure.

That experience shapes how he approaches client work: systematically, with accountability for results, and with a focus on making things that work well, consistently.
